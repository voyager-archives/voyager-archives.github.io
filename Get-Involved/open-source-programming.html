Open Source Programming

Since the beginning, Space Centers have built custom software to match the exact simulation experience they needed to make it feel immersive. From Hand drawn images on cardboard, to Hypercard, Revolution, Cocoa, and now React, space centers have relied on programmers and UI/UX designers to make the experience what it is today.

There are a few projects available to contribute to if you want to program.

  • Thorium (Programmed in Javascript and CSS)
  • Interstellar (Programmed in Javascript, CSS, and HTML5)
  • InfiniD (Programmed (I think?) in C#)
  • Mercury (Programmed in Cocoa and Objective-C)
  • Dreamflight (Programed in Unity3D)
  • Empty Epsilon (Programmed in Lua and C++)
  • Artemis Bridge Sim (Programmed C++)
